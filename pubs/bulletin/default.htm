June 7

Legal Developments: First Quarter, 2016

Three orders were issued this quarter, one for Goldman Sachs Bank USA under the Bank Merger Act, one for Frost Bank under the Federal Reserve Act, and one for Unione di Banche Italiane, S.p.A. under the International Banking Act.

March 17

Legal Developments: Fourth Quarter, 2015

Ten orders were issued this quarter, one for Royal Bank of Canada, Baylake Corp., and BB&T Corporation under Section 3 of the Bank Holding Company Act, one for Community Bank System, Inc. under Sections 3 and 4 of the Bank Holding Company Act, one for Farmers Bank of Northern Missouri under the Bank Merger Act, one for Chemical Bank under the Federal Reserve Act, one for Royal Bank of Canada, Korea Exchange Bank, and Taiwan Business Bank, Ltd. under the International Banking Act, and one for Synchrony Financial under the Home Owners' Loan Act.

January 28

Legal Developments: Third Quarter, 2015

Seven orders were issued this quarter; one for DIT Group, Inc., Banner Corporation, First Horizon National Corporation, and Empresas Juan Yarur SpA under Section 3 of the Bank Holding Company Act; one for M&T Bank Corporation under Section 4 of the Bank Holding Company Act; one for PacWest Bancorp under Sections 3 and 4 of the Bank Holding Company Act; and one for Auburn State Bank under the Federal Reserve Act.
