Conversation with the Chairman

Federal Reserve Chairman Ben S. Bernanke participated in a live webcast of a town hall meeting with educators on Thursday, September 30, 2010 from 2:30-3:30 p.m. EDT. During this session, Chairman Bernanke addressed teachers' questions about the Federal Reserve and the economy. A webcast of this session was available on UStream, and the session can now be viewed on our YouTube channel.
