Commitment to Diversity

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System values 'the diverse backgrounds of its employees and relies on strong teamwork to shape independent viewpoints into effective practices and policies. We provide equal opportunity for all individuals in an inclusive and respectful environment that allows our employees to fully use their individual talents to advance the mission of the Board most effectively.

The ability to develop and carry out our responsibilities is a direct result of the contributions of a highly talented, diverse workforce.

Work/Life Benefits

The Board attracts and retains its employees by offering a highly competitive benefits package. A recent study indicated that Board employees are significantly more satisfied with their benefits package compared with employees at other organizations. In addition to the traditional health and life insurance benefits, the Board provides its employees with an array of complementary services and programs which may include:

  • flexible work schedules;
  • telecommuting;
  • transportation subsidies;
  • dental, vision, and health benefits;
  • domestic partner benefits;
  • a dependent care referral program;
  • a health unit and fitness center;
  • a credit union; and
  • a successful recognition program that rewards individuals and teams for their contributions and achievements.

Professional Development

We use both internal and external training courses to provide professional development opportunities to help our employees increase their knowledge and acquire new skills. The Board supports employee educational development through an academic assistance program.

Employee Committees

To promote inclusiveness, the Board encourages its employees to expand their awareness and to participate in any of several employee committees. These groups provide valuable input to Board programs and also sponsor cultural events.

Recruiting

Recruiting and retaining a highly motivated, well-trained, and diverse workforce is necessary to achieve the Board's mission and goals. Our recruiting efforts are sustained by strong professional relationships with an array of colleges and universities, professional organizations, and associations. We also sponsor a number of internships, a cooperative education program and a visiting scholars program.
