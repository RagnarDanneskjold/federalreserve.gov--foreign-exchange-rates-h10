Electronic Request Form

Before you make a request, please review the frequently requested information that is available in the Reading Rooms, the information on fees in Fees and Waivers, and Tips for FOIA Requesters. You may use the form below to submit your FOIA request.

Privacy Act Statement

Collection of this information is authorized by the Freedom of Information Act and the Privacy Act, 5 U.S.C. 552 and 552a. The information will be used to process your request for information under the Freedom of Information Act. The information you provide may be disclosed (1) to another federal government agency having records within the scope of your request, (2) to the news media and the public, and (3) to a congressional office in response to an inquiry made at your request. Supplying this information is voluntary, but failure to provide all of the information may cause a delay in the processing of your request or may result in the Board's inability to process your request.

* Required field: The information provided to the Board on this form is used for responding to your request. For further information, please see our privacy statement.
