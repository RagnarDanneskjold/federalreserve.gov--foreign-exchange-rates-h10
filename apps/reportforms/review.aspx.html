Data Collections Currently under Review by the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System

The Federal Reserve information collections listed below are currently under review, awaiting implementation, or have been recently approved and implemented. The Federal Reserve review process is in accordance with the specifications of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. As part of that process, we have published a request for public comment in the Federal Register. For each information collection listed below you may select:

  • the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) supporting statement, which describes in detail the data collection and any proposed change
  • proposed changes, which are illustrated in the draft reporting form and instructions
  • any supplemental information or attachments
  • the initial Federal Register notice (a request for public comment on the data collection and any proposed change)
  • the final Federal Register notice (an announcement of Board approval of the data collection)
