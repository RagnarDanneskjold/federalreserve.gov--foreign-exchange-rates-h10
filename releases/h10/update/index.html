Daily Update

Daily updates are posted Monday through Friday on this site. 

 Effective January 1, 2009, the Federal Reserve Board is discontinuing publication of the H.10 Daily Update, 
 which provides U.S. dollar exchange rates against other currencies certified for customs purposes by the 
 Federal Reserve Bank of New York and summary measures of the foreign exchange value of the dollar.  
 The Federal Reserve Bank of New York also has issued notification that it will discontinue publication 
 of these foreign exchange rates on its web site.  

 The Federal Reserve Board will still make the certified exchange rates available.  Effective January 5, 2009,
 the Board will publish the daily exchange rate data in a weekly version of the H.10 release, reinstituting the
 weekly publication, which had been discontinued in May 2006.  The webpage that links to historical data from 
 the H.10 release will also be updated once per week to pick up the daily data from the previous week.
