Structure Data

There are two reports showing the same structure and asset information for each U.S. office, but in different orders. Offices located in Puerto Rico, American Samoa, Guam, the Virgin Islands and other U.S.-affiliated insular areas are excluded.  The first report lists the offices by institution type. The second report is by the home country of the foreign bank.

Each report shows asset totals and subtotals for the categories displayed.

Code definitions appear at the end of each report.

Reports are available in Screen Reader HTML format and the underlying data are also available in ASCII format.

Share Data

This report shows the assets, loans, and deposits of the U.S. offices of foreign banking organizations.  Reporting categories are 1) U.S. branches and agencies of foreign banks; 2) U.S. commercial bank subsidiaries of foreign banking organizations; and 3) U.S. commercial banking subsidiaries of U.S. banking organizations.  The sum of these three categories provides U.S. totals for both domestic and foreign banking organizations.  These totals are used as the denominator in calculating a percentage for the three categories. The data are in millions of dollars and as a percentage (share) of all banking offices in the United States.
